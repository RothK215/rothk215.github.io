Latitude vs. Max Temperature

Summary

The following graph represents the relationship between a City's Latitudal coordinates and Temperatures recorded a the time. Temperaturse start reaching below 0 as latitude reaches 30. Before 35 latitude, more cities are recorded as you can see the points gater near the 20-30 fahrenheit line. Compared to the cities above 35 latitude, we see the points spaced out more by latitude coordinates and less congested as cities below 35 latitude. Above 35 latitude, the points also represents a sudden drop in temperature as latitude increases, a strong negative slope forms.
